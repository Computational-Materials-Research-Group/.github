## About Us

The Computational Materials Research Group is a self-funded Research and Development group, part of **AI Fab Lab**. Our mission is to revolutionize materials science and manufacturing through the strategic application of Artificial Intelligence and data-driven methodologies.

We are committed to changing the course of data-driven material science and manufacturing by developing innovative computational tools, advanced simulation techniques, and machine learning algorithms that accelerate materials discovery and optimize manufacturing processes.

## Research Focus

Our research encompasses several key areas in computational materials science:

- **AI-Driven Materials Discovery**: Machine learning models for predicting material properties and discovering novel materials
- **Molecular Dynamics Simulations**: Advanced LAMMPS-based simulations for understanding material behavior at atomic scale
- **Friction and Tribology**: Computational modeling of friction, wear, and surface interactions
- **Welding Process Simulation**: Modeling friction stir welding and other advanced joining processes
- **Data-Driven Manufacturing**: Optimization of manufacturing processes using computational intelligence
- **Materials Informatics**: Building databases and leveraging data analytics for materials design

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from the materials science and computational research community! Here's how you can get involved:

1. **Fork** the repository you're interested in
2. **Create** a feature branch (`git checkout -b feature/AmazingFeature`)
3. **Commit** your changes (`git commit -m 'Add some AmazingFeature'`)
4. **Push** to the branch (`git push origin feature/AmazingFeature`)
5. **Open** a Pull Request

Please ensure your code follows our coding standards and includes appropriate documentation.
